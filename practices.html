Breathing Exercises

Harness the power of breath to reduce stress, increase energy, and improve focus with our guided breathing exercises.

4-7-8 Breathing

A calming breathing technique that helps reduce anxiety and promote relaxation.

Breathe In
Inhale for 4
Instructions:
  1. Inhale through your nose for 4 counts
  2. Hold your breath for 7 counts
  3. Exhale through your mouth for 8 counts
  4. Repeat the cycle

Box Breathing

A simple technique that helps improve focus and reduce stress by equalizing your breath.

Inhale
Inhale for 4
Box Breathing Steps:
  1. Inhale for 4 counts
  2. Hold for 4 counts
  3. Exhale for 4 counts
  4. Hold empty for 4 counts
