Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and wellness programs.

How do I get started with Pure Wellness?

We recommend starting with our free wellness assessment to understand your current health status and goals. From there, we can recommend the best services and programs for your needs.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes! We offer both in-person and virtual consultations for all our services. Virtual sessions are conducted via secure video calls and are just as effective as in-person meetings.

What should I expect in my first session?

Your first session will include a comprehensive health assessment, goal setting, and development of a personalized wellness plan. We'll also answer any questions you have about our approach.

Do you accept insurance?

We work with most major insurance providers for our mental health services. For other services, we offer flexible payment plans and package deals to make wellness accessible.

How long does it take to see results?

Results vary depending on your goals and commitment level. Most clients notice improvements in energy and mood within 2-4 weeks, with more significant changes occurring over 3-6 months.

Can I change or cancel my program?

Absolutely! We understand that life changes and needs evolve. We offer flexible program modifications and have a 30-day cancellation policy for most services.
